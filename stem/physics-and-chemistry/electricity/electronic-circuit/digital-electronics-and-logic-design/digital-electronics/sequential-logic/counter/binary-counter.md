# Synchronous Binary Counter
- ### Number of States ($n$-bit)：$`2^n`$
- ### Truth Table ($3$-bit)
    |State|Data Ouput($Q$)|
    |:---:|:---:|
    |$S_0$|000|
    |$S_1$|001|
    |$S_2$|010|
    |$S_3$|011|
    |$S_4$|100|
    |$S_5$|101|
    |$S_6$|110|
    |$S_7$|111|
- ### State-Transition Table
    |Current State|Next State|
    |:---:|:---:|
    |$S_i$ ($0\leq i<m-1$)|$S_{i+1}$|
    |$S_{m-1}$|$S_0$|
    - $`m=\text{Number of States}=2^n`$
