# Forms of the Equation of Plane
- ### Parametric form
    - ### $`\begin{cases}{x=x_1+α_1t}\\{y=y_1+α_2t}\\{z=z_1+α_3t}\end{cases},~t\in R`$
- ### Symmetric form
    - ### $`\frac{x-x_1}{α_1}=\frac{y-y_1}{α_2}=\frac{z-z_1}{α_3}`$
- ### [Two-Plane form](#two-plane-form-1)
