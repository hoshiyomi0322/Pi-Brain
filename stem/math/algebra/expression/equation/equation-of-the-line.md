# Parameters of Equation of the Line
- ### Slope：$`m=\frac{Δy}{Δx}=\tan{θ}=-\frac{a}{b}=\frac{α_2}{α_1}`$
    - Angle of Inclination：$`θ=\text{angle between the line and the } x\text{-axis (positive angle)}`$
- ### Intercept：$`\left(x_0,~0\right),~\left(0,~y_0\right)`$
- ### Vector
    - #### [Normal Vector](../../function-and-equation.md#normal-vector)：$`\left(a,~b\right)`$
    - #### [Direction Vector](../../function-and-equation.md#direction-vector)：$`\left(α_1,~α_2\right)`$
- #### Point：$`P\left(x_1,~y_1\right)`$

# Forms of the Equation of the Line
- ### General form (Normal form)
    - ### $`ax+by=c`$
- ### Parametric form
    - ### $`\begin{cases}{x=x_1+α_1t}\\{y=y_1+α_2t}\end{cases},~t\in R`$
- ### Slope-Intercept form
    - ### $`y=mx+y_0`$
- ### Intercept form
    - ### $`\frac{x}{x_0}+\frac{y}{y_0}=1`$
- ### Point-Slope form
    - ### $`y-y_1=m\left(x-x_1\right)`$

# Equations of two Lines：$`L_1,~L_2`$
- ### $`\tan{θ}=\tan{\left(θ_1-θ_2\right)}=\frac{m_1-m_2}{1+m_1m_2}`$
    - #### Angle between two lines：$`θ=θ_1-θ_2`$
    - Angle between two lines = Angle between the [Normal Vectors](#normal-vector) of the two lines
    - #### [Angle of Inclination](#angle-of-inclination)：$`θ_1,~θ_2`$
    - #### [Slope](#slope)：$`m_1,~m_2`$
- #### $`\text{If }L_1\perp L_2,~\text{then }m_1m_2=-1`$
