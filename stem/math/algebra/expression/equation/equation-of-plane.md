# Parameters of the Equation of Plane
- ### Slope
    - ### $`m_x=\frac{Δz}{Δx}=-\frac{a}{c}`$
    - ### $`m_y=\frac{Δz}{Δy}=-\frac{b}{c}`$
- ### Intercept：$`\left(x_0,~0,~0\right),~\left(0,~y_0,~0\right),~\left(0,~0,~z_0\right)`$
- ### Vector
    - ### [Normal Vector](../expression.md#normal-vector)：$`\left(a,~b,~c\right)`$
    - ### [Vectors in the Plane](../expression.md#direction-vector)：$`\left(α_1,~α_2,~α_3\right),~\left(β_1,~β_2,~β_3\right)`$
- ### Point：$`P\left(x_1,~y_1,~z_1\right)`$

# Forms of the Equation of Plane
- ### General Form (Normal Form)
    - ### $`ax+by+cz=d`$
- ### Parametric Form
    - ### $`\begin{cases} {x=x_1+α_1t+β_1s}\\{y=y_1+α_2t+β_2s}\\{z=z_1+α_3t+β_3s} \end{cases},~s\in R,~t\in R`$
- ### Intercept Form
    - ### $`\frac{x}{x_0}+\frac{y}{y_0}+\frac{z}{z_0}=1`$
- ### <span id="point-slope-form">Point-Slope form</span>
    - ### $`z-z_1=m_x\left(x-x_1\right)+m_y\left(y-y_1\right)`$
- ### Cartesian Form 
    - ### $`\begin{vmatrix} x-x_1&y-y_1&z-z_1 \\ α_1&α_2&α_3 \\ β_1&β_2&β_3 \end{vmatrix}=0`$
    - $`\left(x-x_1,~y-y_1,~z-z_1\right)\perp \left(\left(α_1,~α_2,~α_3\right)\times \left(β_1,~β_2,~β_3\right)\right)`$
