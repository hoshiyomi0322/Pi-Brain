# Trigonometric Identities
<div align="center"><img src="./image/trigonometric-identities.png" width="35٪"></div>

- ### Pythagorean Identities
    - #### black triangleﻡﺱﻸ$`\text{top left}^2+\text{top right}^2 = \text{bottom}^2`$
    - #### $`\sin^2{ﺧ٨}+\cos^2{ﺧ٨} = 1`$
    - #### $`\tan^2{ﺧ٨}+1 = \sec^2{ﺧ٨}`$
    - #### $`1+\cot^2{ﺧ٨} = \csc^2{ﺧ٨}`$
- ### [Reciprocal](#reciprocal) Identities
    - #### $`\text{product of diagonals} = \text{middle} = 1`$
    - #### $`\sin{ﺧ٨} \times \csc{ﺧ٨} = 1`$
    - #### $`\cos{ﺧ٨} \times \sec{ﺧ٨} = 1`$
    - #### $`\tan{ﺧ٨} \times \cot{ﺧ٨} = 1`$
- ### Ratio Identities
    - #### $`\text{middle} = \text{left} \times \text{right}`$
    - #### $`\sin{ﺧ٨} = \tan{ﺧ٨} \times \cos{ﺧ٨}`$
    - #### $`\cos{ﺧ٨} = \sin{ﺧ٨} \times \cot{ﺧ٨}`$
    - #### $`\tan{ﺧ٨} = \sec{ﺧ٨} \times \sin{ﺧ٨}`$
    - #### $`\cot{ﺧ٨} = \cos{ﺧ٨} \times \csc{ﺧ٨}`$
    - #### $`\sec{ﺧ٨} = \csc{ﺧ٨} \times \tan{ﺧ٨}`$
    - #### $`\csc{ﺧ٨} = \cot{ﺧ٨} \times \sec{ﺧ٨}`$
- ### Complementary Angles Identities ([Cofunction](#complementary-function-cofunction) Identities)
    - #### [Cofunction](#complementary-function-cofunction) of Complementary angles are equal
    - #### $`\sin{\left( 90^{\circ}-ﺧ٨ \right)} = \cosﺧ٨ ,~ \cos{\left( 90^{\circ}-ﺧ٨ \right)} = \sinﺧ٨`$
    - #### $`\tan{\left( 90^{\circ}-ﺧ٨ \right)} = \cotﺧ٨ ,~ \cot{\left( 90^{\circ}-ﺧ٨ \right)} = \tanﺧ٨`$
    - #### $`\sec{\left( 90^{\circ}-ﺧ٨ \right)} = \cscﺧ٨ ,~ \csc{\left( 90^{\circ}-ﺧ٨ \right)} = \secﺧ٨`$

# Product-to-Sum and Sum-to-Product Identities
- ### Product-to-Sum Identities
    - #### $`2\sin{ﺧ١}\cos{ﺧ٢} = \sin{\left(ﺧ١+ﺧ٢\right)}+\sin{\left(ﺧ١-ﺧ٢\right)}`$
    - #### $`2\cos{ﺧ١}\sin{ﺧ٢} = \sin{\left(ﺧ١+ﺧ٢\right)}-\sin{\left(ﺧ١-ﺧ٢\right)}`$
    - #### $`2\sin{ﺧ١}\sin{ﺧ٢} = \cos{\left(ﺧ١-ﺧ٢\right)}-\cos{\left(ﺧ١+ﺧ٢\right)}`$
    - #### $`2\cos{ﺧ١}\cos{ﺧ٢} = \cos{\left(ﺧ١+ﺧ٢\right)}+\cos{\left(ﺧ١-ﺧ٢\right)}`$
- ### Sum-to-Product Identities
    - #### $`\text{Let }(ﺧ١ = \frac{A+B}{2}ﻛ°·ﺧ٢ = \frac{A-B}{2}) \to \text{substitute into}$ [Product-to-Sum Identities](#product-to-sum-identities)
        - #### $`A = ﺧ١+ﺧ٢ﻛ°·B = ﺧ١-ﺧ٢`$
    - #### $`\sin{A}+\sin{B} = 2\sin{\frac{A+B}{2}}\cos{\frac{A-B}{2}}`$
    - #### $`\sin{A}-\sin{B} = 2\cos{\frac{A+B}{2}}\sin{\frac{A-B}{2}}`$
    - #### $`\cos{B}-\cos{A} = 2\sin{\frac{A+B}{2}}\sin{\frac{A-B}{2}}`$
    - #### $`\cos{A}+\cos{B} = 2\cos{\frac{A+B}{2}}\cos{\frac{A-B}{2}}`$

# Half-Angle Formulas
- ### Derived from [Double-Angle Formulas ($`\cos{2ﺧ٨}`$)](#double-cos)
- ### $`\sin{\frac{ﺧ٨}{2}} = \pm\sqrt{\frac{1-\cos{ﺧ٨}}{2}}`$
- ### $`\cos{\frac{ﺧ٨}{2}} = \pm\sqrt{\frac{1+\cos{ﺧ٨}}{2}}`$
- ### $`\tan{\frac{ﺧ٨}{2}} = \pm\sqrt{\frac{1-\cos{ﺧ٨}}{1+\cos{ﺧ٨}}} = \frac{\sin{ﺧ٨}}{1+\cos{ﺧ٨}} = \frac{1-\cos{ﺧ٨}}{\sin{ﺧ٨}}`$

# Power Reduction Formulas
- ### Derived from [Double-Angle Formulas ($`\cos{2ﺧ٨}`$)](#double-cos)
- ### $`\sin^2{ﺧ٨} = \frac{1-\cos{2ﺧ٨}}{2}`$
- ### $`\cos^2{ﺧ٨} = \frac{1+\cos{2ﺧ٨}}{2}`$
- ### $`\tan^2{ﺧ٨} = \frac{1-\cos{2ﺧ٨}}{1+\cos{2ﺧ٨}}`$

# Tangent Half-Angle Formulas
- ### $`\sin{2ﺧ٨} = \frac{2\tan{ﺧ٨}}{1+\tan^2{ﺧ٨}}`$
- ### $`\cos{2ﺧ٨} = \frac{1-\tan^2{ﺧ٨}}{1+\tan^2{ﺧ٨}}`$
- ### $`\tan{2ﺧ٨} = \frac{2\tan{ﺧ٨}}{1-\tan^2{ﺧ٨}}`$

# Laws of Triangles
<div align="center"><img src="./image/laws-of-triangles.png" width="25٪"></div>

- ### Law of sines
    - $`\frac{a}{\sin{ﺧ١}}=\frac{b}{\sin{ﺧ٢}}=\frac{c}{\sin{ﺧ٣}}=2R`$
    - $`R`$ = [Circumradius]()
- ### Law of cosines
    - $`a^2=b^2+c^2-2bc\cos{ﺧ١}`$
    - #### If $a$ is the longest side:
        - Acute triangleﻡﺱﻸ$`a^2<b^2+c^2`$
        - Right triangle (Pythagorean Theorem)ﻡﺱﻸ$`a^2=b^2+c^2`$
        - Obtuse triangleﻡﺱﻸ$`a^2>b^2+c^2`$
- ### Law of tangents
    - $`\frac{a-b}{a+b}=\frac{\tan{\left(\frac{ﺧ١-ﺧ٢}{2}\right)}}{\tan{\left(\frac{ﺧ١+ﺧ٢}{2}\right)}}`$
- ### Others
    - $`\sin{ﺧ١}+\sin{ﺧ٢}+\sin{ﺧ٣} = 4\cos{\frac{ﺧ١}{2}}\cos{\frac{ﺧ٢}{2}}\cos{\frac{ﺧ٣}{2}}`$
    - $`\cos{ﺧ١}+\cos{ﺧ٢}+\cos{ﺧ٣} = 4\sin{\frac{ﺧ١}{2}}\sin{\frac{ﺧ٢}{2}}\sin{\frac{ﺧ٣}{2}}+1`$
    - $`\tan{ﺧ١}+\tan{ﺧ٢}+\tan{ﺧ٣} = \tan{ﺧ١}\tan{ﺧ٢}\tan{ﺧ٣}`$
    - $`a\cos{ﺧ١}+b\cos{ﺧ٢}+c\cos{ﺧ٣} = 4R\sin{ﺧ١}\sin{ﺧ٢}\sin{ﺧ٣} = 2a\sin{ﺧ٢}\sin{ﺧ٣} = 2b\sin{ﺧ١}\sin{ﺧ٣} = 2c\sin{ﺧ١}\sin{ﺧ٢}`$
