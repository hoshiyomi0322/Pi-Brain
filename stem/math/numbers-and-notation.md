    |Open Interval|$`(a,~b)`$|$`\{x\mid a<x<b\}`$|
    |Close Interval|$`[a,~b]`$|$`\{x\mid a\le x\le b\}`$|
    |Half-open Interval|$`(a,~b]`$|$`\{x\mid a<x\le b\}`$|
    |Half-open Interval|$`[a,~b)`$|$`\{x\mid a\le x<b\}`$|
