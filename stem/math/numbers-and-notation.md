# Extremum 
|Notation|Definition|
|:---:|:---:|
|$\max{x}$|Maximum|
|$\min{x}$|Minimum|
|$\mathop{\text{argmax}}{~x}$|Argument of the Maximum|
|$\mathop{\text{argmin}}{~x}$|Argument of the Minimum|
- ### eg
    - $f(x)=\begin{cases} {f(1)=35}\\{f(2)=69}\\{f(3)=24} \end{cases}$
    - $\max{f(x)}=69$
    - $\min{f(x)}=24$
    - $\mathop{\text{argmax}}{~f(x)}=2$
    - $\mathop{\text{argmin}}{~f(x)}=3$
